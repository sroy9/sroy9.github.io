About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Robust Robotics Group at CSAIL, MIT. I am working with Prof Nick Roy on problems at the intersection of natural language processing and robotics. Prior to MIT, I received my Ph.D. from the department of Computer Science at University of Illinois, Urbana Champaign, under the supervision of Prof. Dan Roth. Even prior to that, I got my B.Tech degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur.
